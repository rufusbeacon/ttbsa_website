Who We Are

Every journey through the Bible is personal — and yours is as unique as you are. Whether you want to explore Scripture from the beginning or jump in anywhere, we offer study plans designed to fit your rhythm.

We start in Genesis (Old Testament), move into Matthew (New Testament), and continue weaving through all 66 books of the Bible — thoughtfully, systematically, and book by book — helping you discover the full story of God's Word.

About Thru the Bible

At the core of what Thru the Bible is and what we do is the conviction that the Bible is true and trustworthy. We believe God uses His Word uniquely in each of our lives to transform the way we think, believe, and live.

How does He do this? We believe that, in part, He does this by telling us His own story: the story of His love, His redemption — a story that He planned and put into place before our time began. At its heart, it's the story of how He has opened the way for us to know Him, to love and obey Him, to grow deeper day by day in the ways we trust Him.

It's the story Thru the Bible tells every day on the air and invites people to listen.

Dr. J. Vernon McGee, our founder and teacher, loved to tell the story more than anything else in the world. He began teaching on the radio in 1941, hoping the San Gabriel Valley and the Los Angeles area would respond to the wonders of the Bible, simply told.

And to his surprise, people listened. Because not only is the Bible a good story, and Dr. McGee a good storyteller, but through the Bible is also the primary way God draws us to Himself.

While teaching Hebrews, Dr. McGee said, "The only way you and I are going to stay close to Jesus is to stay close to His Word... That's why we're spending time in the Word of God, and the reason I read letters from people on the air. They say 'For the first time I found out about the joy of the Lord.'"

That’s why it’s not unusual for many longtime repeat travelers on “the Bible Bus.” People come aboard and stay because they grow closer to Jesus each trip—and there’s always room for more.

But we must be clear: people don’t come aboard because of Dr. McGee. They come because Dr. McGee points them to Jesus. He would be surprised at the global response to his simple preaching. We honor his courage to answer God’s call, but we give glory to God for the generations changed by His Word.
